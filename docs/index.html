FAQ

Good to know

Is Beemo free?

Yes — completely free. Send and receive as much as you want, with no limits and no subscription.

What can I send?

The tab you're on, a group of tabs, links, and text notes. You can also choose to carry the form values you typed on a page — never passwords.

Is it private?

Items are shared only with the friend you pick. We use your Google account just to identify you to friends, and never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy.

Do I need an account?

Just sign in with Google so friends can find you. That's it — no payment, no card, nothing else.

Which browsers work?

Beemo is a Chrome extension and works on Chrome (desktop). Both you and your friend need it installed.
